About Alpha Beta App

Alpha Beta App is a demonstration of a modern, full-stack web application template.
It features a clean, glassmorphism-inspired UI and a robust backend structure.

Our Mission

To provide developers with a beautiful, functional, and easy-to-extend starter kit for their next big project.
